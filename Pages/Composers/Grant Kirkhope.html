Biography

Grant Kirkhope was born July 10th, 1962 in Edinburgh, Scotland. He was originally employed by Rare in October 1995, making this his first job as a video game composer. Before this, he played for two bands. The first game he contributed music for was Killer Instinct 2.

In his career, Grant Kirkhope has worked for quite a few well-known gaming companies including Rare, Nintendo, Microsoft, and Playtonic Games. He has also done freelance work.

Kirkhope's musical style has varied a lot over the years. Some of his earlier works such as Banjo-Kazooie, Banjo-Tooie, and Donkey Kong 64 showcase his ability to write highly memorable and catchy tunes. His more recent works showcase his other bigger and more grandeur orchestral style as seen in Mario & Rabbids: Kingdom Battle, Yooka-Laylee, and Banjo Kazooie: Nuts & Bolts.
