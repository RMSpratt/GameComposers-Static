Biography

Go Ichinose is a Japanese composer currently working at Game Freak. He originally joined the company in 1993 to gain some programming experience, but was recruited by Junichi Masuda to the sound team to help with Pokémon Gold and Silver. Prior to this, Ichinose's first contribution as a composer for Game Freak was the puzzle game Bazaru de Gozaru no Game de Gozaru

Ichinose was placed in music classes early on, but was more interested in practicing songs he liked, opposed to those he was told to practice. Even when he joined Game Freak initially, he had no innate desire to make music for the company. However, things took a wide turn as he would go on to be credited in several mainline Pokémon games.
