Biography

Asuka Hayazaki, formally Asuka Ohta, has been a composer at Nintendo since 2003. The first game she worked on with the company was The Legend of Zelda: Four Swords Adventures where she composed most of the soundtrack with some help from Koji Kondo.

Hayazaki since then has worked on several games in the Super Mario and Legend of Zelda series, through providing original compositions and arrangements of songs.
