Biography

Asuka Hayazaki is a composer who has been working for game development company Nintendo since 2003. She is probably best known for her contributions in the Super Mario and The Legend of Zelda series.

The first game Hayazaki worked on with Nintendo was The Legend of Zelda: Four Swords Adventures where she composed most of the soundtrack with some help from Koji Kondo. Since then, she has worked on several games in the Super Mario and Legend of Zelda series, providing both original compositions and arrangements of previous songs.
