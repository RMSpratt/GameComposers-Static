Biography

Yoko Shimomura is a Japanese composer and arranger that has lent her hand in many widely-known video games and video game series, including Kingdom Hearts, Mario & Luigi and Super Smash Bros. She first started composing music for games after being hired as a student, and was previously in school to become a piano instructor.

Shimomura's musical style is very diverse, and she often incorporates multiple in her works to suit the gameplay and environment. Her works can range from cheery and upbeat, to dark and emotional. When composing music, she often goes through a lot of trial and error to fine tune each track.

Her favourite track composed is currently "Dearly Beloved" from the Kingdom Hearts series. Despite this, she is quoted as saying that she found composing for this series to be quite intimidating early on, as it required many arrangements of well-known beloved Disney songs.
